Publications

Publishing

All accepted and presented papers in the conference will be published in the conference proceedings and will be indexed in Scopus, Ei Compendex and other major databases.

Scopus is a robust academic database designed to assist researchers, academics, and institutions in efficiently accessing and analyzing scholarly literature. It indexes research papers, journals, conference proceedings, and patents, making it a valuable tool for tracking and evaluating academic work. This platform enables researchers to discover, access, and cite the latest research in various disciplines, contributing significantly to academic progress.

Compendex is an engineering and technical research database that serves as a valuable resource for professionals in engineering and related fields. It contains a wide range of technical literature and research papers, making it a go-to tool for staying updated on the latest developments in various engineering disciplines, from mechanical to electrical engineering.

Selected and Extended Papers of the Conference will be published in the following Journals

01
SN Computer Science, Springer Nature, SJR: Q1

SN Computer Science, published by Springer Nature, is a leading journal in computer science, covering topics such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and more. It holds a Q1 ranking according to the SJR, indicating its high impact in the field.

02
Frontiers in Digital Health, SJR Q1

Frontiers in Digital Health, led by Field Chief Editor Prof Björn Wolfgang Schuller (Imperial College London, UK), explores the ways in which innovative digital tools and technologies can transform modern healthcare.

03
International Journal of Critical Computer-Based Systems, Inderscience Publishers

IJCCBS specializes in engineering mission-critical computer-based systems (CBS) with complex, distributed architectures. It addresses challenges in design and analysis, emphasizing the importance of context awareness, environmental factors, and interactions with diverse systems.

04
International Journal of Information Security and Privacy - IJISP

IJISP focuses on information security and privacy. The journal addresses key issues in safeguarding information, emphasizing the latest advancements in security technologies and privacy measures. It provides insights into securing data and ensuring privacy in an evolving technological landscape.

05
Interdisciplinary Description of Complex Systems - INDECS

INDECS is dedicated to the interdisciplinary study of complex systems. The journal explores the dynamics, structures, and behaviors of complex systems, fostering a comprehensive understanding across various disciplines. It serves as a platform for the exchange of insights and research on the intricate nature of complex systems.
